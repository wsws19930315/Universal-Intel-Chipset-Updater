[Cyan] SUPPORT DEVELOPMENT
[Cyan] ===================

 Universal Intel Chipset Device Updater provides automated hardware
 detection and Intel INF deployment for thousands of Intel platforms,
 including many systems no longer actively maintained by OEM vendors.

 The hardware ID database is continuously updated to ensure accurate
 device recognition across both legacy and modern Windows installations.

 If this tool saved you time, improved device identification, or helped
 keep older hardware updated, please consider supporting development.
